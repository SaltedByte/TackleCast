========================================
  TackleCast - Quick Start Guide
========================================

CONTROLS
--------
  Escape    Open / close the settings menu
  F11       Toggle fullscreen

That's it! TackleCast is designed to stay out of your way.
Just plug in your capture card, launch TackleCast, and play.

WINDOWS SMARTSCREEN
-------------------
Windows SmartScreen may block TackleCast from launching because
the app is not code-signed. This is normal for open-source apps
built with Python/PyInstaller.

If SmartScreen blocks the app, try clicking "More info" then
"Run anyway." If that option is not available, you may need to
temporarily disable SmartScreen:

  1. Open Windows Security (search "Windows Security" in Start)
  2. Go to App & browser control
  3. Click "Reputation-based protection settings"
  4. Turn off "Check apps and files"
  5. Launch TackleCast
  6. Turn the setting back on when you're done

This only needs to be done once. After the first launch, Windows
should remember that the app is safe.

You can adjust video resolution, frame rate, audio devices,
and volume from the settings menu (Escape).
